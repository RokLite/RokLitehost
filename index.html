  • FEATURES

    Here are some key features of RokLite:

    • Track the statistics of a provided tiktok video of your choice such as Views, Likes, Comments, and Shares.
    • Track the statistics of a provided twitter video of your choice such as Impressions, Likes, Comments, and Reposts.
    • Track the statistics of all submitted videos as a whole
    • Track your place on the leaderboard among other users using RokLite

  • COMMANDS

    Explore the following commands supported by RokLite:

    • /setup: Setup RokLite in your discord server
    • /add: Add your TikTok or Twitter account you want to track statistics of
    • /tiktok: Add a TikTok video that you want to track statistics of
    • /twitter: Add a Twitter video that you want to track statistics of
    • /stats: Review the statistics of all the videos you have added
    • /leaderboard: Check your place on the leaderboard
